Services

Gas Pipeline Construction

The pipeline construction process is executed with great expertise and efficient processes to attain excellent deliverability at every pre-FEED and construction phase.

Gas Distribution

Our gas distribution project aims to meet off-takers natural gas demand with their respective volumes through pipeline tie-points. We strive at providing clean and affordable gas to meet gas utilization demands in industries, complexes, and residential communities within and beyond the gas distribution franchise area.

Pipeline Operations and Maintenance

Aegon’s overall objective in pipeline maintenance and monitoring is to ensure the high-level safety and operability of pipelines. This is to secure and aid the optimum distribution of gas across all facets. Our maintenance activities include pipeline integrity testing, leak surveys, repair of leak points, line section replacement, amongst others.

Energy Distribution and Trading

We are committed to energy deliverability through the supply and trading of consumable gas across the nation and beyond. We have an experienced team of professionals with in-depth expertise in gas energy network systems.
